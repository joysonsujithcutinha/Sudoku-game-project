About Me

Hello! I am Joyson Sujith Cutinha, a passionate designer, passionate about game design and logic puzzles. I created a Sudoku game that supports a variety of grid sizes—4x4, 6x6, and 9x9—for both beginners and experienced players. My goal with this project was to combine my love of coding with the challenge of creating a fun educational game. I hope players enjoy the way of thinking and problem solving that Sudoku offers as much as I do! In my spare time, I enjoy exploring new programming languages, tackling complex algorithms, and participating in coding challenges. I'm excited to continue to develop my skills and take on more interactive projects in the future!
